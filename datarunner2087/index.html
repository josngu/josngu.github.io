The file naming convention should look similar to the following: MoAG_FileSubject_2087M10D01.jpg

Ex. MoED_... (Ministry of Education)
List of valid abbreviations:
AG (Agriculture)
DE (Defense)
ED (Education)
EM (Employment)
EN (Energy)
HE (Health)
IN (Infrastructure)
TR (Transportation)
